#3 - Make you talking head videos look professional

Hook Structure
Value promise
Number-based hook (3 ways)

Body Structure
Tip 1 explanation
Tip 2 explanation
Tip 3 explanation


ENGLISH VERSION

Audience:
Content creators, YouTubers, and people who make talking head videos.

Hook:
There are three simple ways to make your talking head videos look much better.

Body:
Number one — place the lights behind the subject and point them toward the camera. This creates depth and makes the subject stand out.
Number two — place the light on the shadow side and about 45° behind the subject. This adds a more cinematic and professional look.
Number three — place the light right behind the subject and point it toward the background. This helps separate the subject from the background and improves the overall visual quality.

CTA:
Try these lighting setups in your next video and see the difference.

HINGLISH VERSION

Audience:
Content creators, YouTubers aur jo log talking head videos banate hain.

Hook:

Body:

Number two — light ko shadow side par aur subject se 45° peeche rakho. Isse video ka look zyada cinematic lagta hai.
Number three — light ko subject ke bilkul peeche rakho aur background ki taraf point karo. Isse subject background se alag aur clear dikhai deta hai.
